Regular Verbs

SUBJECT -AR (HABLAR) -ER (COMER) -IR (VIVIR)

VOS HABLÁS COMÉS VIVÍS
ÉL – ELLA – USTED HABLA COME VIVE
NOSOTROS (AS) HABLAMOS COMEMOS VIVIMOS
VOSOTROS (AS) HABLÁIS COMÉIS VIVÍS

  • CAMINAR (to walk)
  • COMPRAR (to buy)
  • ESCUCHAR (to listen)

  • HABLAR (to speak)

  • PRACTICAR (to practice)
  • TRABAJAR (to work)
  • USAR (to use)
-ER Verbs (to ...)
  • APRENDER (to learn)
  • BEER (to drink)
  • COMER (to eat)
  • COMPRENDER (to understand)
  • CREER (to believe)
  • LEER (to read)
  • VENDER (to sell)
  • VER (to see)
-IR Verbs (to ...)
  • ABRIR (to open)
  • ASISTIR (to attend)
  • COMPARTIR (to share)
  • ESCRIBIR (to write)
  • RECIBIR (to receive)
  • SUBIR (to go up)
  • VIVIR (to live)

The Present Simple describes an action that happens in the present moment.

Learning Spanish: Levels & Resources

Spanish learning is a journey! I offer structured support for every level, from complete beginner (A1) to mastery (C2). Each level is based on the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages) and includes tailored materials and guidance.

Levels Explained

  • A1 (Beginner): Basic phrases, introductions, everyday needs.
  • A2 (Elementary): Simple conversations, directions, daily routines.
  • B1 (Intermediate): Handle travel, work, and social situations. Narrate experiences.
  • B2 (Upper Intermediate): Discuss abstract topics, opinions, and more complex grammar.
  • C1 (Advanced): Express yourself fluently, understand nuance, use idioms.
  • C2 (Mastery): Near-native proficiency, academic and professional Spanish.

What You Get at Each Level

  • Custom PDF guides and worksheets
  • Audio and video practice
  • Real-life articles and news
  • Homework and quizzes for reinforcement
  • Personalized feedback and progress tracking

Not sure of your level? I’ll help you assess your Spanish and create a plan that fits your needs. Contact me for a free assessment.

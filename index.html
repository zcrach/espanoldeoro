About me

I’m Salvador De Oro, a certified Spanish teacher from Colombia with over 4,900 lessons taught and a degree in Foreign Language Teaching (Spanish, English, and French) from Universidad del Atlántico.

  • I focus on creating a relaxed, student-centered environment where you’re free to make mistakes, ask questions, and grow naturally.
  • My goal? To get you talking, thinking, and understanding Spanish in a way that feels real, not rehearsed.
  • I use videos, audio, articles, and custom exercises to keep lessons engaging and effective.
  • Whether you're a total beginner or have been learning Spanish for a long time, we’ll build a clear path to fluency, together.
